Collection Changes:
. shifts: is now going to have an embedded employees array.
. employees: the employeeId field will go away.
. assignments: this group will be removed.

Persistence Layer Updates:
- Removed functions:
    .getAssignments()
    .createAssignment()
- Modified functions:
    .getShifts() → changed to give out shifts with the employees array.
    .assignEmployeeToShift() → will no longer use assignments to update the shift document; it will update the shift document directly.

Business Logic Updates:
. The shift.employees array will be directly updated with assignment operations.
. Any queries should be done with ObjectId rather than employeeId or shiftId.

Impact of Embedded Arrays:
. Push and pull operations will be needed to make updates.
. There should be no multiple entries of employees.
. Firing a worker involves amending all shifts.

Potential Risks:
. Errors when converting to ObjectId
. Bad references in the process of migration.
. Redundant elements in the employees array.
. Inability to completely eliminate legacy assignment logic.
